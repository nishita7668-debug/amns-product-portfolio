🟦 Coated Steel — Value-Added Segment

1.1 Metallic-Coated Steel

  • Galvanised Steel (GI)
    • Forms: Sheet, Coil
    • Use: Automotive, appliances, infrastructure
  • Galvanised Corrugated Sheets (GC)
    • Use: Roofing, wall cladding
    • High durability in outdoor environments
  • Magnelis (Zn-Al-Mg Coated Steel)
    • Advanced corrosion-resistant coating
    • Superior life vs traditional galvanised steel
    • Forms: Sheet, Coil

1.2 Pre-Painted / Colour-Coated Steel

  • Optigal (Premium Portfolio)
    • Optigal Prime
    • Optigal Pinnacle
    • High-end architectural and industrial applications
  • Vibrance
    • Color-coated steel offering
    • Focus: aesthetics + durability
  • Pre-Painted Galvanised Steel (PPGI)
    • Forms: Coil, Sheet
    • Applications: appliances, roofing, construction
  • Pre-Painted Profile Sheets
    • Use: Roofing and wall cladding

1.3 Special Coated Products

  • Optima
    • Specialised coated product variant
    • Likely positioned for niche/high-performance applications

Strategic Role

  • Highest margin segment in AMNS portfolio
  • Focus on value-added steel vs commodity products
  • Key for automotive, infrastructure, and renewable sectors
